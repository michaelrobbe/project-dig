1. Wake up cold, wet, in the mist. Only option is [[Explore]]. Land is Unexplored/Explored/Owned, Maybe meters^2?
  2. Explore enough, and [[Establish Campsite]] becomes an option; ==Resource section== opens up
  3. Message "Need to get warm". [[Gather Wood]] Action. Resources have an Owned/Max/MaxAvailable
  4. Continue exploring and [[Gather Fiber]] from nearby plants, is an option
  6. Gather enough [Wood], and [Fiber] and [[build fire]] becomes an option
  7. While {Fire} is active, [wood] is consumed, but rate of [[Gather]] is x2-3
  8. [Fiber] + [Dry Wood] = {Torch}, torch consumes fiber, but rate of [[Explore]] is x2-3
  9. After enough exploring, [[Gather Stone]], becomes an option
  10. Reveal the area as very swampy
  12. ==Buildings== and ==Upgrades== become available with stone reserves
  13. Upgrade {Fire} => {Campfire} => {Bonfire} OR {Cooking Fire}
  14. Build Tools out of [Stone], [Wood], and [Fiber]
  15. [[Drain Campsite]] in order to access buildings [[Build Foundation]] => Small/Med/Large Structure => Assign Structure Role
